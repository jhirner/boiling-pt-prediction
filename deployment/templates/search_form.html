Boiling Point Predictor

This tool implements a machine learning model to predict the atmospheric pressure boiling point of a given molecule.

Note: Predictions are valid for molecules with boiling points in the range of 400 - 700 K (127 - 427 °C).

Please draw a structure to get started.
